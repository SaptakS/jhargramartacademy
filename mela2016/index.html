ABOUT



Rong Mati Manush is the annual exhibition-cum-fair of Jhargram Art Academy. This year its the 27th anniversary of the occassion.
This year, the theme of the event is "Eka Noe Ek Saathe, Tai Anonde Dine Raate" which translated to english is synonymous to the famous english phrase "United we stand, Divided we fall". This year we thus look forward to celebrate and promote unity and integrity amongst us all living beings which is essential for a healthy and peaceful environment. So, come join us in this occasion of merry making at the beginning of the new year 2016.
